Returns Policy

Returns & Refunds Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

At SentriX, we strive to provide the best learning experience for our participants. This policy outlines our refund and return procedures for our internship programs.

Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • 7-Day Money-Back Guarantee: Full refund within 7 days of enrollment if you haven't started the program
  • Technical Issues: Refund if we cannot resolve technical problems preventing program access
  • Program Cancellation: Full refund if we cancel a program before it begins
  • Service Unavailability: Refund if our services become unavailable for extended periods

Non-Refundable Situations

The following situations are not eligible for refunds:

  • After 7 days from enrollment date
  • After program content has been accessed or downloaded
  • If program has been completed
  • Violation of our Code of Conduct
  • Change of mind after program commencement
  • Personal circumstances preventing participation

Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us at support@sentrix.in.net
  2. Include your enrollment details and reason for refund
  3. We will review your request within 3-5 business days
  4. If approved, refund will be processed within 7-10 business days

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued through the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 7-10 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 3-5 business days
  • Digital Wallets: 1-3 business days

Partial Refunds

In exceptional circumstances, partial refunds may be considered:

  • Medical emergencies with documentation
  • Technical issues preventing program completion
  • Service quality issues

Certificate Replacement

For certificate-related issues:

  • Free replacement for technical errors on our part
  • Small fee for reprints due to user error
  • Processing time: 3-5 business days

Contact Information

For refund requests or questions about this policy:

Email: support@sentrix.in.net

Address: Gandhi Nagar, Upper Worli, Worli, Mumbai - 400018

Response Time: Within 24 hours during business days
